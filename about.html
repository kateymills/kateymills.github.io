KATEY WYNNE
is an Illustrator, graphic designer, & motion graphics artist based in Austin, TX. She is a senior at Texas A&M University studying for her BS in Visualization and minor in Business. She has a tuxedo cat named Louise, she loves to dance, enjoys cartoons -her favorite being Bob's Burgers- and signing things with smiley faces :) Her work consists of digital and physical graphics, website design, illustration, animation, and video editing. She draws a lot of funny little characters and edits short videos of her friends and life. She is first and foremost a typography nerd, and loves to experiment with bold colors and unique type.
